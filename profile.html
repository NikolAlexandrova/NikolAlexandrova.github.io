Why did I choose ICT?

ICT is a very interesting program that allows me to be flexible. Thanks to it, I don't need to focus on only one field of the IT world. It enables me to explore more and combine my interests. For example, I like programming but I also enjoy the part in which I have to choose how the interface of an app should look or how a website should be done. Additionally, I am motivated to study this program, because I don't feel the time passing by. Moreover, it doesn't make me stressed and I am feeling like I am studying while having fun. Furthermore, another good motivation for me is that it enables me to travel and work from different parts of the world.
