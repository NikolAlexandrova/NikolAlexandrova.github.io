Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

Go to https://hz.mynetpay and then click on Webprint, select your file and click on upload.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Check this webpage

How can I buy something (like when I sign up for the IT introduction event) on the HZ web shop?

Log into this webshop https://webshop.hz.nl

How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

Go to Self-Service Portal then you can make a reservation and reserve a room.

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

Go to the reception because they need to activate your card, after that you can reveal your HZ pass at the barriers next to APV in Middelburg, then you will be able to park in the lot for free:)
