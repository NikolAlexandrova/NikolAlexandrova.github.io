Study Choice

What did I choose to study?

I am studying ICT in the Netherlands.

Why did I choose to study it?

I chose to study ICT because it is interesting and allows me to be flexible. I don't need to focus on only one field of the IT world. It enables me to explore more and combine my interests. For example, I like programming but I also enjoy the part in which I have to choose how the interface of an app should look or how a website should be done. Additionally, the profession that this program offers me enables me to travel around the world without having to sit in an office the whole day.

Do I have any experience with it so far?

I started studying Computer science in eleventh grade so I've been studying it for two years now. Along with my studies in school, I had personal programming lessons, where I got more deeply into the programming languages Java and SQL. Moreover, I also had an internship at a company, which helped me learn how to work with JSON files and how to convert them to Java objects.

What are my plans after the study?

I would like to work in an IT company either as a software engineer or business IT consultant. With time I would like to achieve a higher position and transfer to a bigger company.
