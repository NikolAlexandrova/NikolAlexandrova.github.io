What type of projects/work do they do?
 Salesforce is a leading customer relationship management (CRM) platform that offers a wide range of cloud-based services and solutions to help businesses manage their relationships and interactions with customers. Some of the work they do is connected with Sales and Lead Management, Marketing Automation, Customer Service and Support, Analytics and Business Intelligence, Custom Application Development, Community and Portal Development, Integration, AI and Machine Learning, Industry-Specific Solutions and Nonprofit and Philanthropic Initiatives.
How big are they in number of staff? (Small business, multinational, etc.)
 Salesforce is a multinational company. It operates globally and has a presence in many countries around the world. Salesforce is known for its cloud-based customer relationship management (CRM) solutions and other enterprise software products, and it serves customers from various industries and regions. In September 2021, Salesforce had more than 49,000 employees worldwide.
What do they tell you about their company culture?
 Salesforce is known for its strong company culture, which is often highlighted in its messaging and core values. Some key aspects of Salesforce's company culture include:
Ohana (Family): Salesforce often refers to its employees and customers as part of the "Salesforce Ohana," emphasizing a family-like environment where employees and customers are treated with care and respect. Innovation: The company values innovation and encourages employees to think creatively and develop new solutions. Equality: Salesforce is committed to promoting equality and diversity within the workplace. It has taken active steps to address issues related to gender, pay equity, and LGBTQ+ rights. Sustainability: Salesforce is dedicated to sustainability and environmental responsibility, striving to minimize its environmental impact. Philanthropy: Through its 1-1-1 model, Salesforce donates 1% of its product, 1% of its equity, and 1% of its employees' time to charitable causes. Employee Wellbeing: Salesforce invests in employee wellness and offers various programs and initiatives to support employee health and well-being. Customer-Centric: The company prioritizes the success of its customers and aims to build lasting relationships with them. Transparency: Salesforce promotes transparency in its business practices and decision-making.

What do they tell you about their company culture?
 Apple Inc. promotes a company culture that values innovation, creativity, and excellence. While the specifics of a company's culture can evolve and vary, here are some elements that have historically been associated with Apple's company culture:
Innovation: Apple is renowned for its commitment to innovation and pushing the boundaries of technology. The company encourages employees to think creatively and develop groundbreaking products.
User-Centric Approach: Apple's culture emphasizes a strong focus on delivering exceptional user experiences. This customer-centric approach is reflected in the design and usability of its products.
Design Excellence: Apple places a high value on product design and aesthetics. The company's design teams work to create products that are not only functional but also visually appealing.
Secrecy and Confidentiality: Apple has been known for its culture of secrecy, especially when it comes to product development. This culture of confidentiality is intended to protect upcoming product releases.
