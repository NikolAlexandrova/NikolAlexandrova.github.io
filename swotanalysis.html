Personal SWOT-Analysis

Strengths

One of my strengths is my curiosity. It comes in handy when I don't know how to write a certain part of my code and need to do research. Moreover, I am flexible, which is very useful when my code doesn't work, because I try to think of other ways to achieve my final goal.

Weaknesses

I struggle with organization. It has never impacted my performance until now, however, it is hard for me to plan the structure of my code beforehand.

Threats

One of the obstacles I face is that I wait until the last moment to do my assignments. This could be crucial for the success of my studies. That's why I will be focus on that and try to change it now that I at university.

Opportunities

If I prioritise my weaknesses it will allow me to work on them more and improve in these fields. This could be very helpful when it comes to working in a company. This also includes stepping outside of my comfort zone. If I do that I would be able to make my weaknesses an opportunity.
